Quick tips before interview

  1. Always research the airline & its values.
  2. Arrive 15 minutes early—dress neat and professional.
  3. Speak slowly, smile, keep answers short & structured.
  4. Highlight teamwork, safety mindset and customer care examples.
  5. Ask 1–2 thoughtful questions at the end (training, culture).
